# Equipment Maintenance SOP: Centrifugal Pump

## 1. Purpose

This Standard Operating Procedure defines maintenance requirements,
fault diagnosis procedures, and corrective actions for centrifugal pumps
in pharmaceutical manufacturing processes.

## 2. Scope

Applies to all centrifugal pumps in production areas P1–P4,
including API synthesis, granulation, and filling lines.

## 3. Fault Diagnosis

### 3.1 Seal Leakage

**Symptoms**: Visible liquid dripping from the shaft seal area, increased
vibration at shaft coupling, unusual humming noise, pressure drop below
normal operating range of 2.5–3.0 bar.

**Causes**: Seal wear from abrasive media, improper installation,
shaft runout exceeding 0.05 mm, or operation outside design parameters
(temperature above 80°C or pressure above 4.0 bar).

**Corrective Action**: Stop pump immediately. Replace mechanical seal
per Drawing PM-PUMP-021. Verify shaft runout with dial indicator.
Document in equipment log.

### 3.2 Overheating

**Symptoms**: Pump body temperature exceeding 70°C, acrid smell from
motor housing, automatic thermal cutout triggered.

**Causes**: Insufficient flow causing dry running, blocked suction
strainer, bearing failure, or motor winding fault.

**Corrective Action**: Isolate pump immediately. Check suction strainer.
Inspect bearings. Do not restart until root cause is confirmed.

### 3.3 Loss of Prime

**Symptoms**: No flow output despite pump running, cavitation noise
(rattling or crackling), decreased motor amperage.

**Causes**: Air entrainment in suction line, suction valve not fully
open, NPSH available less than NPSH required (typically 3.5 m).

**Corrective Action**: Vent pump casing through priming port.
Verify suction valve is fully open. Check for air leaks in suction piping.

## 4. Preventive Maintenance Schedule

| Interval  | Task |
|-----------|------|
| Weekly    | Visual inspection for leaks, vibration check |
| Monthly   | Lubricate bearings (Grease: SKF LGHP 2) |
| Quarterly | Replace mechanical seal, inspect impeller |
| Annual    | Full overhaul, bearing replacement |

## 5. Critical Safety Limits

- Maximum operating pressure: 4.0 bar
- Maximum temperature: 80°C
- Minimum flow rate: 20% of rated capacity
- Maximum seal face temperature: 60°C

Any operation outside these limits is a GMP deviation and
must be reported to Quality within 2 hours.
